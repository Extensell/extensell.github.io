Privacy Policy

The Service is available for merchants who own an e-commerce account and may be used by downloading our application through your e-commerce account (the “Application”).

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Notice (the “Notice”) explains our privacy practices for the Service. The Notice also describes the rights and options available to you with respect to your personal information.

PERSONAL DATA WE PROCESS

Information we obtain from your e-commerce platform.

The Application is available only to merchants who own an e-commerce account.

While using the Application, we gain access to the following information of your Shopify store: product list, discounts, gift cards, Shopify plan. Depending on your choice to use some features in the Application, we may also gain access to order history and order details.

Analytics

When you use the Application, we use third party tools such as Google Analytics, Amplitude and Hotjar to collect information about your use of the Application. For example, we may record the frequency and scope of your use, action taken while using the Application and the interactions you make with the Application.

HOW WE PROCESS AND USE PERSONAL DATA

We process your data for the following purposes:

  • To operate the Service and provide its features and functionality.

  • To develop and improve the Service.

SHARING YOUR INFORMATION

We are committed to protecting your personal information and we do not sell your personal information to third parties. Here’s how we handle your information:

  • We share your information with our service providers helping us to operate the Service.
  • If you violate the law, we will share your information with competent authorities.
  • If we are required to disclose your information by a judicial, governmental, or regulatory authority.

KEEPING CUSTOMER INFO SAFE

We store customer info in a secure Google cloud database.

  • We encrypt this info when it’s stored and when it’s sent somewhere.
  • Only our app can get to the data, and we keep track of who does what with it.
  • We keep customer info for up to one year.

INFO WE COLLECT WHEN YOU VISIT OUR SITE

We gather some details about your device, like the type of web browser and your IP address. We use cookies, log files, web beacons, tags, and pixels to do this.

CHILDREN’S USE OF OUR SERVICES
